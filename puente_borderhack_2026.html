Connecting 2 million people to the resources they need

The El Paso–Ciudad Juárez metro is one of the largest binational communities on Earth. But healthcare, legal aid, education, and social services are fragmented across two countries, two languages, and two bureaucracies. Most residents don't know what help exists — or how to access it.
